The different options available:

## Roleplay <br>

**Purpose:** Simulates conversational scenarios (sales calls, customer service, small talk).

**Interaction:** Shorter, turn-based prompts with back-and-forth dialogue; often expects adaptive responses.

**UI/Tools:** On-screen scenario prompts, suggested responses, quick-turn timers.

**Feedback focus:** Naturalness, response relevance, conversational flow, filler words, tone.

## Interview <br>

**Purpose:** Simulates job or formal interviews (behavioral and technical Q&A).

**Interaction:** Sequential question set (often preset or randomized); single-answer responses per question.

**UI/Tools:** Question bank, recommended answer structure (e.g., STAR), timed answers, optional follow-ups.

**Feedback focus:** Content structure, completeness, clarity, examples, confidence, pacing.

## Presentation <br>

**Purpose:** Practice longer, prepared monologues (talks, slides, demos).

**Interaction:** Extended continuous speaking with focus on structure and delivery.

**UI/Tools:** Slide or prompt display, longer timers, recording playback with timeline.

**Feedback focus:** Pacing, volume/energy, eye contact, transitions, overall organization, audience engagement.
