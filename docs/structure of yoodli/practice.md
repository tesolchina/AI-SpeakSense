The practice tab has 3 following options that you can practice:
1. Roleplay
2. Presentation
3. Interview
<br>
<br>


# 1. Roleplay
The roleplay tab has 2 further subtabs:

### 1. Choose a roleplay:
You can choose different types of roleplays from a drop down menu and you can also select a persona for the roleplay partner

You have the following options of roleplays to select from:

1. Cold sales call
2. Inbound customer discovery
3. Manager skills training
<br>
etc.

Lastly there is a `Create Your Own` button at the bottom of the drop down list which takes you to the builder tab `Builder > Roleplays > Create new`


### 2. Quick conversation:
you can choose a custom topic, roleplay partner and personality from a drop down menu to design a conversation

- you have the following conversation topics to select from:
1. Giving colleague constructive feedback
2. Giving a sales pitch
3. Small talk
<br>etc.

- you can also choose who you are roleplaying with:
1. Colleague
2. Manager
3. Mentor

- you can also choose different personalities for your roleplay partner to practice all possible real life scenarios:
1. Professional
2. Friendly
3. Stressed
<br>etc.
<br>
<br>


# 2. Presentation
In this section you can give a 45 minute speach or practice a presentation and AI will analyze your facial expressions, vocabulary, gestures, language used etc.
<br>
<br>


# 3. Interview
The interview tab also has further 2 subtabs

### 1. Choose a roleplay:
- you can select the type of interview you want to practice:
1. Technical Interview
2. Behavioral Interview

- you can also select a persona for your interviewer

- you can also select the role you are applying for so the interview questions are related to your intended job:
1. General
2. Product Manager
3. Software Developer
<br>etc.

- you can also select the company you are applying for, for more personalized interview
1. Google
2. Amazon
3. Adobe
<br>etc.

There is a `Create Your Own` button at the bottom of the drop down list which takes you to the builder tab `Builder > Interviews > Create new`

Lastly here is a `Create from a job description` button as well which takes allows you to input the details of a specific job and AI creates a personalized interview specific to that job. you need to input the following information:

1. Company name
2. Default role
3. Description:<br>
Add a description of your roleplay below. (sort of like a caption to describe what this interview is about)
4. Context: <br>
Brief the AI on the interview as though it were the interviewer. It will navigate the interview based on the context entered.
5. Persona:<br>
Add the personas you can practice this roleplay with
6. Assessment Rubrics
7. Add potential questions you expect to be asked from you
8. You can also choose to describe at what point should the interview be ended. time constraints etc.

you also have the option to autofil this information by uploading a pdf or a URL link
